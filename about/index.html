About DevQueens

A women-only hackathon designed to unlock creativity, confidence, and technical excellence.

Our Mission

DevQueens Women's Hackathon is dedicated to empowering women in technology by providing a focused space for learning, building, and leadership. Participants team up to solve real-world challenges, explore new tools, and present solutions with support from mentors and peers.

What Makes It Special

Inclusive by Design

Women-first participation that supports safe collaboration and shared growth.

Innovation Focused

Challenge tracks encourage bold ideas and practical prototypes in one day.

Community Powered

Organized by Women Empowerment Committee with HackUnion as collaboration partner.
